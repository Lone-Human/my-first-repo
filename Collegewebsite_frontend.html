Welcome to Kisan P.G. College, Bahraich (U.P.)

The institution was established on July Ist 1960, affiliated to Gorakhpur University by the famous Freedom Fighter and Senior Cabinet Minister of U.P. late Thakur Hukum Singh Visen the founder president of the college for the promotion of higher education in the district. The then District Magistrate, Sri Girish Chander Chaturvedi I.A.S. as Vice president-I; Ex M.P. Late Pandit Bhagwaan Deen Vaidya as the Vice president-II of the college; Sri Sardar Jogindar Singh Ex. Governor of Rajasthan; Sri Babu Magan Bihari Lal Srivastava Advocate & Secretary of the college; Sri Bhagwati Singh as Joint Secretary; Dr. J.B. Singh the Founder Principal of the college and many other leading personalities of district extended their whole hearted co-operation for the institution.

Library & Resource Center

Our central library is a hub of knowledge, housing a vast collection of textbooks, reference materials, academic journals, periodicals, magazines, and newspapers. We also provide access to digital resources and e-journals.

The library offers a quiet and conducive environment for study and research, equipped with reading areas and computer facilities.

Gymnasium

We encourage physical fitness and well-being. The college has a well-equipped gymnasium with modern fitness machines and equipment, accessible to both students and staff during specified hours under the guidance of a trainer.

Hostel Facilities

To accommodate students from distant locations, the college provides separate, secure, and comfortable hostel facilities for boys and girls. Hostels are equipped with essential amenities, including mess facilities providing hygienic food, common rooms, and recreational options.

Admission to the hostel is based on specific criteria and availability.

Seminar Hall

The college features a modern, air-conditioned seminar hall equipped with state-of-the-art audio-visual technology, including projectors and sound systems. It serves as an ideal venue for conducting seminars, workshops, guest lectures, conferences, and other academic and cultural events.

Sports & Recreation

Kisan P.G. College promotes sports and games for the all-round development of students. We have extensive grounds and facilities for various outdoor sports like cricket, volleyball, football, kabaddi, and athletics, as well as provisions for indoor games like badminton, table tennis, chess, and carrom.

Inter-college and intra-college tournaments are organized regularly.
