Frequently Asked Questions

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in time is 3:00 PM and check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability and potential fees.

Is parking available at the hotel?

Yes, we offer secure valet parking for our guests at a daily rate. Self-parking options are also available nearby. Please contact the concierge for details.

Do you allow pets?

We understand pets are part of the family! We welcome well-behaved small dogs (under 25 lbs) in designated pet-friendly rooms. A pet fee and specific policies apply. Please contact us directly to book a pet-friendly room.

What dining options are available?

The Grand Horizon boasts several dining experiences: our signature fine-dining restaurant 'Celeste', the casual 'Horizon Bistro' for all-day meals, the chic 'Sky Bar' for cocktails and light bites, and 24/7 in-room dining service.

Is there a fitness center and spa?

Absolutely! We have a state-of-the-art Fitness Pavilion open 24/7 for guests. Our Tranquil Harmony Spa offers a range of treatments and relaxation facilities. Advance booking for spa treatments is recommended.
